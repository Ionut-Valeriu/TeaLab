Teas

Common hogweed

Best proportion 2 teaspoons 100 ml water
Method of Preparation Soak two teaspoons of the plant (leaves and fruits) in 100 ml of spring water, from evening to morning. After macerating the tea, strain the liquid. Then, over the remaining plant in the pot, pour 150 ml of boiling water. After another 15 minutes, add the water in which the plant was macerated to the obtained infusion.
Method of Administration Drink three cups a day, one cup 15 minutes before the main meals.
Contraindications Do not exceed the recommended dose for daily consumption. The product does not contain potentially allergenic ingredients. Contraindicated for women during pregnancy.

Meadowsweet

Best proportion 1 spoonful 200 ml water
Actions Helps to detoxify the body (supports diuresis and the normal functioning of the sweat glands, helps to eliminate toxic products of metabolism), supports digestion and the osteoarticular system.
Method of Preparation Put a spoonful of shredded aerial parts in 200 ml of boiling water, let it infuse for 15 minutes, then drink it warm for 10-15 minutes.
Method of Administration Drink 2 cups a day in the morning and in the evening, after the main meals. Do not exceed the recommended dose for daily consumption.

Lavender

Best proportion 2 teaspoons 250 ml water
Contributes to Maintaining normal blood pressure, promoting healthy sleep, helpful in cases of anxiety and irritability, supports digestive comfort during temporary stress.
Preparation and Usage Add 2 teaspoons of the plant to 250 ml of boiling water, let it steep for 15 minutes, strain, and drink. Drink 3 cups of tea per day, 30 minutes before main meals.
Precautions and Contraindications Not to be consumed by pregnant or breastfeeding women.
